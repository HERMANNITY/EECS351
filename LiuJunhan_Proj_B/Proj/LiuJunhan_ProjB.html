Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Instruction:

Mouse Interaction: 1)Double click on the canvas to adjust the view. 2)Drag on the screen to rotate the Tetrahedron.

Keyboard Interaction: Press "r" to return the original status. Press "b" and "l" to adjust the size of the rabbits head.

Press "w" "a" "s" "d" "," "." to adjust the position of your eyes step by step. "shift" and "control" to adjust the speed of your movement.

In the flying mode, Press "u" and "j" to pitch up and down, press "h" and "k" to turn left and right.

Press "1" "2" "3" "4" "5" "6" "7" "8" to adjust the 6 frustrum.

Button Interaction: Press Yaw left and right, Pitch up and down to yaw up and down and pitch up and down

Press follow to see the world with the end of the wierd joints.

Press fly to start and stop the flying mode.
