Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Instruction:

Mouse Interaction: 1)Double click on the canvas to draw new balls. 2)Drag on the screen to move the left shape.

Keyboard Interaction: Press "s" to stop animation. Press "r" to return the original status. Press "b" and "l" to adjust the size of the rabbits head. Press arrows to adjust the position of the rabbits head.

Press the buttons under the canvas to adjust the speed.
